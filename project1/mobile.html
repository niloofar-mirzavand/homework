Mobile phone, mobile phone or phone, are tools for sending and receiving sound and image through radio communication in a wide geographic area. A mobile phone, or cell phone, is a device used to connect to a mobile phone network. The first wireless phone was made in 1973 with a weight of nearly 200 grams. There are different generations of mobile phones and they are produced and sold by different manufacturers. Some of the major mobile phone manufacturing companies in the world include Nokia, Huawei, Samsung, LG, Motorola, Sony, Apple, Xiaomi, HTC, Google Pixel, etc.
